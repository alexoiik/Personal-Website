Few Things

My name is Alexandros Oikonomou, "alexoiik", and i am 22 years old, so i still have a lot to learn and discover. I was born in a beautiful and picturesque city in Greece, called Ioannina. The last 3 years i have been living in Thessaloniki, where i study. Spending time there outside with my friends and having some fun is the best feeling. I am quite extroverted, i do not like loneliness and i usually prefer to spend my time going out, at least for a coffee or a walk, or "at worst" to chill at home with a good company!
